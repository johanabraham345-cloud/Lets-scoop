Our Story

A small scoop shop with a big menu

Let's Scoop started as a simple idea: a neighbourhood spot where an ice cream run doesn't have to end at ice cream. Alongside our sundaes and hand-scooped flavours, you'll find shakes, falooda, juices and a proper snack menu — so there's always a reason to stop by, whatever you're in the mood for.

Every order is made fresh at the counter. No shortcuts, no syrup pumps from a machine — just real fruit, real toppings, and a scoop size that doesn't hold back.
