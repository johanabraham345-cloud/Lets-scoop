Our Story

A little scoop of joy, made right here in Kochi

Let's Scoop started with one simple idea: dessert should feel like a treat, every single time. Tucked into Edappally, our little café churns out hand-crafted ice creams, thick shakes and Kochi's favourite falooda alongside a full menu of burgers, wraps, momos and fresh juices — so there's always a reason to stop by, whether you're craving something sweet or something to share.

Every order is made fresh when you ask for it — no shortcuts, no sitting around. Just clean ingredients, generous portions and a team that genuinely loves seeing you smile at the first bite.

  • Fresh-made ice creams & shakes, no pre-mixes
  • Full food menu — burgers, wraps, momos & more
  • Dine-in, takeaway, delivery & no-contact delivery
